George Brown College is located in Toronto, Ontario. It currently had three campuses - 
Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront. The Computer Programming and Analysis Program is
held at the Casa Loma campus and online.

George Brown College offers a wide variety of programs where you could participate in an
apprenticeship, earn a diploma, or even a degree! They also have many post graduate programs
for people looking to additional studies in fields of their interest.
