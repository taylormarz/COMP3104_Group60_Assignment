Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)* is important for many reasons, among those reasons are:
1. Helps manage risks effectively via continuous monitoring.
2. flexibility and adaptability via constant improvement and innovation
3. Improves project management as it fosters understanding of scope, objectives and requirements
4. Automation can be incorporated to reduce manual errors
5. Allows for faster delivery with such a streamlined process

*SDLC consists of planning, analysis, design, implementation, testing/integration, and maintenance, In that order.
